Welcome to APAC Service Center & Insurance Services

Welcome to APAC Service Center & Insurance Services. We are a dedicated team committed to enrolling individuals in health insurance programs and offering utility bill assistance. Our mission is to ensure that every member of our community has access to the necessary services to lead a healthy and stable life.

Our Services

  • Health Insurance Program Enrollment:

    We offer comprehensive assistance to help you enroll in the right health insurance program. Whether it's through covered California or other state and federal programs, our team will guide you through every step to ensure you and your loved ones are covered.

  • Utility Bill Assistance:

    Struggling with utility bills? We provide support to help you manage and pay your utility bills to avoid service interruptions. Our counselors will work with you to find the best solutions and programs available.

  • Community Support Services:

    Beyond health and utility assistance, we also offer various community support services, including food assistance programs, job training, and more. Our goal is to empower our community members to achieve self-sufficiency and well-being.
